Airbnb business analysis using a data science approach
Feedback

Feedback on team dynamics

1. How effectively did your team work?

We collaborated effectively, dedicating at least one hour each week to discuss our project, delegate tasks, share ideas, set deadlines, and address challenges. The team maintained a supportive and solution-focused dynamic, actively brainstorming and providing feedback on each other's contributions. While some team members were highly motivated-sometimes creating pressure for others to find additional time to contribute and generate new ideas-we successfully met all the assignment criteria and ensured the quality of our work.

2. Were there any behaviours of your team members which were particularly valuable or detrimental to the team?

We worked collaboratively as a team, sharing new ideas and supporting one another throughout the project. Weekly brainstorming sessions allowed us to discuss challenges in depth and develop effective solutions. To streamline our work, we divided the group into two subgroups: a writing team and a coding team. Our team leader, Natali Nikolic, facilitated our meetings efficiently by preparing detailed agendas. I personally organized the first group meeting and led one session within my coding subgroup. Our focus was always on mutual support, striving to develop the best code to address compelling business questions, which were then professionally articulated by the writing team.

3. What did you learn about working in a team from this project that you will carry into your next group/team experience?

This was my first opportunity to organize a team meeting, including our initial group meeting and a session within the coding subgroup. I also presented my results, along with the work of another team member, to an audience with diverse backgrounds, making it a unique and enriching experience. This challenge significantly enhanced my leadership skills and boosted my confidence in communicating and coordinating with a team.
